SKILLS AND JOBS


TEAM MEMBER 1 - APP DESIGNER

The App Designer will be committed to the project part time and will be responsible for designing the user interface for the application and designing it so that it can work across multiple platforms including iOS and Android. The App Designer needs to collaborate with the developers throughout the design process, ensuring that they’ll be able to meet the end goal of the designs. The user should be able to stay in the loop of the latest standards, changes, and trends in mobile application design. Finally, this user should be able to provide creative ideas and inspiration that can contribute to the aesthetics of the ever changing graphical user interface to make it appealing to potential and current users. The designer will work with the app developers to determine user needs and apply those needs to the final functional design and prototype of the application.

SKILLS, EXPERIENCE & REQUIREMENTS:

  • Obligation: Part-time
  • Strong graphic design skills, with a good understanding of typography, intuitive layouts and palate development.
  • Expert level skills with Adobe Photoshop, and prototyping software such as Figma or LucidChart.
  • Proven ability to deliver high quality standards to customers.
  • Deep understanding of both the Apple and Android ecosystems, guidelines and requirements.
  • Good communication skills.
  • 1+ years experience with app design with proof of previous experience.
  • A team player who can adapt to a rapidly changing environment.

TEAM MEMBER 2 - MOBILE DEVELOPER #1

The app developer should be a computer software engineer whose responsibilities will include creating, testing, and programming the application for mobile phones. The developer will be working in a team and will conceive ideas and concepts for the targeted end users of the application. The developer will meet with the Project Manager and they will work together to determine project requirements and will develop the system to meet these requirements. The mobile developer should be in constant communication with the App Designer to accurately meet each other’s common end needs. The mobile developer will also be required to conduct numerous tests, with targeted end-users, to confirm correct operation and functionality of the application.

SKILLS, EXPERIENCE & REQUIREMENTS:

  • 1+ years of programming experience.
  • Experience with various programming languages such as:
    • HTML
    • CSS
    • JavaScript
    • jQuery
    • PHP
  • Exceptional leadership and organisational skills.
  • Effective communication skills.
  • Creativity.
  • Problem solving skills.

TEAM MEMBER 3 - MOBILE DEVELOPER #2

The second app developer will work under the direction of Mobile Developer #1 and will follow the guidelines set out by the developer and designer to develop the application that is based on the requirements conceived with the Project Manager. The developer will be in constant communication with the development team to accurately meet the established end needs. The mobile developer will also be required to set up a range of tests that will be conducted with targeted end-users, to confirm correct operation and functionality of the application.

SKILLS, EXPERIENCE & REQUIREMENTS:

  • 1+ years of programming experience.
  • Experience with various programming languages such as:
    • HTML
    • CSS
    • JavaScript
    • jQuery
    • PHP
  • The ability to work within a team
  • Effective communication and problem solving skills.

TEAM MEMBER 4 - APP TESTER

The App Tester will hold the traits and characteristics of the intended end-user and will possess at least one year of both functional testing and system testing experience. The App Tester will be monitored by the app development team as they are given instructions to interact with the program to confirm the correct operation and functionality of the application. The App Tester will have no prior knowledge of the application as to not corrupt test results.

SKILLS, EXPERIENCE & REQUIREMENTS:

  • 1+ years of functional testing experience
  • 1+ years of system testing experience
  • Recording and documenting test results.
  • Performing security audits.
  • Generating analyses/final reports of test results.
